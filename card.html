African Penguin

African Penguins are also know as Jackass penguins!

  • Scientific Name: Spheniscus demersus
  • Average Height: 60 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 10 to 27 years
  • Habitat: Southwestern coast of Africa

The African Penguin has a black stripe pattern with black spot on its chest. Pink glands above its eyes become pinker as it gets hotter. Males are larger with larger beaks.
